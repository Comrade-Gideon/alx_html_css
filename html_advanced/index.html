What is Holberton School?

Holberton School is a project-based coding school that offers a full-stack software engineering program focused on hands-on learning and practical experience.

How long is the Holberton School program?

The Holberton School program typically lasts for two years, consisting of intensive project-based learning, with a focus on real-world skills and collaboration.

Is Holberton School only for experienced coders?

No, Holberton School is designed for individuals of varying coding backgrounds, including beginners. The curriculum is structured to accommodate both beginners and those with some coding experience.

Do I need to pay tuition upfront?

Holberton School offers an income share agreement (ISA) model, allowing students to defer tuition until after they secure a job in the field. This means you only pay tuition once you're employed and earning a certain income
